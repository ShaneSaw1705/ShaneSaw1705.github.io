How I will conquer my weaknesses in this course

28 March 2024

My career goal

I have been coding for years for absoloutely free and working jobs I hate in the mean time.

My goal is to reclaim my life by turning my passion in to a career and being able to provide for me and my partner. Dev Academy plays a huge roal in this dream, providing the environment to be a part of a team! Which is something you cant get as a solo however, it is a vital part of getting in to the industry

A big weakness of mine comes when you look beyond the code, I often struggle to understand myself and be able to sell myself. I am hoping that Dev Academy will help bring the best out of me and help me tackle my weaknesses in this domain. and I hope that the Facilitation team will help keep me accountable and be there should I need the help.

As I begin this course, I am aware of my weaknesses and I am determined to overcome them. I have identified the following areas that I need to work on:

  • Time management
  • Ego
  • Discipline
  • My communication skills

Here is my plan to address these weaknesses:

Time management

I will create a study schedule that includes dedicated time for each subject. I will set aside time each day to review the material and complete assignments. I will also use a planner to keep track of deadlines and due dates.

My ego

I will allocate time out of my day to work on my own projects to keep my brain working in a coding environment and continue to grow my skills and knowledge by continueing with my own learning.

Discipline

I often find myself getting side tracked in things I shouldnt be doing, which relates back to the side projects. I think working on my own projects would be a great use of my time to keep developing as a developer. I have a hard schedule to work around due to my current life situation but I am going to dedicate 90 minute blocks throught the day for solid work. Studys show that smaller time blocks of work lead to higher returns in productivity

Communication

I need to take the time to learn more about myself. I will make an effort to spend more of my time reflecting

But its not all bad. I do have alot of strengths in the art of coding

  • I have been a self taught programmer for 10+ years and I have had the opertunity to learn an arsenal of coding languages
  • I have a strong understanding of the fundamentals of programming
  • I have a passion for learning and I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills

By following this plan, I am confident that I will be able to overcome my weaknesses and succeed in this course. I am excited to see how much I will grow and learn over the next few months. I also am going to interact with my class mates more and engage myself more often.
